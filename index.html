What is Blockchain?

By allowing digital information to be distributed but not copied, blockchain technology created the backbone of a new type of internet. Originally devised for Bitcoin and other cryptocurrencies, many developers and industries have been finding other critical uses for Blockchain.

Why join our club?

We will connect you with industry professionals and technical workshops to learn more about this quickly-growing and profitable field. No experience is necessary!

In our Github, you'll find some of the projects and workshops that we've done!
